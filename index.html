Look back and reflect

Overview

Looking back over the first portion of your journey reflect on what you have learned and experienced so far. Each of Reflection will build on the last. They are meant to stimulate thought and contemplation to help guide your journey.


Being able to review progress from an objective stand point is an important skill to have in one's toolbox. Progress can be made in countless ways, however, the perceived amount made is defined by the metrics used to measure it. Try not to limit the amount of progress made by limiting the scope of how you measure your progress.

Assignment

  1. What is a full stack application? (As your currently know it) 
  2. What have you done to achieve your goals?
  3. What did you perceive has been the “hardest” thing so far?
  4. How did you face this challenge?
  5. What do you think will be the “hardest” thing ahead?
  6. How will you face this challenge?
  7. What are some lessons you've learned?
  8. What are the take-aways of your experience?
